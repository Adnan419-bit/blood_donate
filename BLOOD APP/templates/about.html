Our Mission

To create a world where no life is lost due to blood shortage by building the largest, most efficient blood donation network.

BloodConnect bridges the gap between blood donors and those in need through technology, making blood donation accessible, transparent, and life-saving for communities worldwide.

Our Vision

A future where every person in need of blood can find a donor within minutes, regardless of location or circumstances.

We envision a global community of heroes who come together to save lives, supported by cutting-edge technology and unwavering commitment to human welfare.

Blood Donation Facts

One Donation Saves Three Lives

A single blood donation can be separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets, potentially saving up to three different patients.

Every 2 Seconds Someone Needs Blood

Blood is needed for emergencies, surgeries, cancer treatments, and chronic illnesses around the clock.

Only 3% of Eligible People Donate

Despite 38% of the population being eligible to donate blood, only 3% actually do so regularly.

O-Negative is Universal Donor

O-negative blood can be given to patients of any blood type, making it extremely valuable in emergencies.

Emergency Request Guidelines
✓ Use Emergency Request When:
  • Life-threatening situation
  • Surgery scheduled within 24 hours
  • Severe blood loss or trauma
  • Critical medical condition
  • Hospital blood bank shortage
✗ Do NOT Use for:
  • Routine blood tests
  • Planned surgeries (use regular request)
  • Non-urgent medical procedures
  • Blood donation drives
  • False or test requests
What Happens Next?
  1. Your emergency request is immediately broadcast to all compatible donors
  2. Donors receive SMS, email, and push notifications
  3. Interested donors will contact you directly
  4. Our emergency team monitors the request status
  5. You'll receive updates on donor responses
